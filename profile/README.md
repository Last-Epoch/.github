## About Last Epoch

Last Epoch is an action RPG that combines fast-paced combat, deep character progression, and time-travel mechanics across a dark fantasy world. Players journey through multiple eras, exploring changing environments, battling enemies, and shaping their character through skill trees, gear upgrades, and crafting systems.

The game focuses on build customization, allowing players to develop unique playstyles through class specialization, abilities, and item combinations. Loot collection, crafting, and progression systems provide long-term growth while challenging encounters and bosses test player strategies.

With a mix of exploration, combat, time-based storytelling, and character development, Last Epoch delivers a rich action RPG experience built around progression, experimentation, and replayability.
